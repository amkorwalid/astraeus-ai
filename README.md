# Astraeus AI

### The First AI Video Director

Most AI video editors give you features.

Astraeus gives you a video editor that thinks.

Instead of manually cutting clips, adding captions, creating shorts, or learning complex editing software, simply tell Astraeus what you want:

> "Turn this 2-hour podcast into 10 viral shorts."

> "Make this video feel more cinematic."

> "Create a YouTube version and a TikTok version."

> "Remove boring parts and keep only the strongest arguments."

Whether you're using Premiere Pro, CapCut, Final Cut, or any modern editor, the workflow remains the same:

* Learn the software
* Understand timelines
* Perform repetitive edits

AI features have improved editing.

But nobody has reinvented the editor itself.
